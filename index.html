What is GameDex ?

GameDex is a gaming database designed to bring all your favorite titles together in one place. It serves as a comprehensive platform where users can explore the latest game releases, detailed game statistics, and insightful reviews.

Beyond being just a gaming database, GameDex is also the home of an in-depth case study focused on the decline of the stealth genre in modern gaming. Through data-driven analysis and interactive visuals, we explore the slow "death" of stealth games and their place in today's gaming landscape.
