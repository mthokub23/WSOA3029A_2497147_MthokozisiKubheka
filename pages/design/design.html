    User Interface Elements

    Navigation

    Buttons, scrollbars, and links play a crucial role in guiding users.

    • Buttons: For interaction, e.g., the "back to top" button.
    • Scrollbars: For content navigation.
    • Links: Internal navigation for easy access to different sections.

    Media

    Enhancing the visual appeal with images and videos.

    • Images: Representing featured content.
    • Videos: To showcase gameplay and tutorials.

    Data

    Using tables and charts for visualization.

    • Tables: Displaying game information.
    • Charts: For visual storytelling with D3.js.

    Color Palette for GameDex

      Dark Charcoal

      Used for primary backgrounds to create a dark, immersive experience.

      White

      Used for text and icons to ensure readability and contrast.

      Bright Yellow

      Used for buttons and highlights to draw attention to important elements.

      Darker Yellow

      Used for secondary buttons and highlights to provide visual hierarchy.

      Light Grey

      Used for backgrounds and borders to provide a neutral and clean look.

      Dark Grey

      Used for text and icons to ensure readability and contrast.

      Medium Grey

      Used for secondary elements and backgrounds to create depth.

      Slightly Darker Grey

      Used for hover effects and active states to provide visual feedback.

      Modal Overlay

      Used for modal backgrounds to focus user attention on the modal content.

      Green Accent

      Used for success messages and accents to convey positivity and success.
